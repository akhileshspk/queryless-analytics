The Margin Mystery: Recovering Profit in a Volatile Market

How Precision Parts Co. stopped their margin leak by deploying a real-time Dynamic Pricing Engine that responds to market shifts in minutes, not months.

The Business Problem

Precision Parts Co. managed a massive catalog of 2,000 different SKUs. As raw material costs fluctuated wildly due to global inflation, their static pricing model couldn't keep up. By the time their team manually adjusted prices across all sales channels, they had already lost weeks of profitable margin. They were essentially flying blind, only discovering their true margins at the end of the quarter.

7% Overall Net Profit Increase
25% Target Margin Maintained

The QueryLess Solution

We developed a custom Dynamic Pricing Engine tailored to their distribution workflow. We built a data pipeline that scraped competitor pricing and tracked global raw material indices in real-time. This data was fed into a Python-based logic engine that suggested price adjustments daily to maintain a strict 25% net margin.

The solution included a dashboard that highlighted "low-sensitivity" SKUs—niche parts where customers were less likely to react to small price increases—allowing for strategic margin recovery without risking market share.

The Result

The model revealed that for 200 specific SKUs, a 5% price increase went completely unnoticed by the market, immediately boosting the bottom line. Overall, Precision Parts Co. saw a 7% increase in net profit and successfully maintained their target margins through three consecutive cycles of material cost hikes.
